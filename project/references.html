Discover the Peaks of Guatemala

Guatemala is known as the “Land of Eternal Spring,” but it’s also home to some of Central America’s most majestic volcanoes and mountain landscapes. Explore a few of its most iconic peaks below.

Volcán de Pacaya

One of Guatemala’s most active volcanoes, Pacaya offers thrilling hikes and stunning lava views. Located near Antigua, it stands 2,552 meters above sea level.

Volcán Acatenango

Standing at 3,976 meters, Acatenango provides breathtaking views of neighboring Fuego Volcano’s eruptions. It’s a popular overnight trek for adventurers.

Volcán Atitlán

Located on the shores of Lake Atitlán, this 3,535-meter giant creates one of Guatemala’s most iconic landscapes, surrounded by picturesque Mayan villages.

Volcán Tajumulco

The highest peak in Central America at 4,220 meters, Tajumulco offers a challenging climb and an unforgettable sunrise above the clouds.
