Volcano Pacaya

Difficulty: Moderate

Pacaya is accessible from Antigua and offers a dramatic volcanic landscape. Recommended to go with an experienced guide and proper shoes.

Difficulty: Moderate

Beautiful viewpoints over the lake and surrounding volcanoes; best visited during dry season.
