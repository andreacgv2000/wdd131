Guatemala

Guatemala is a beautiful country located in Central America. It is known as the "Land of Eternal Spring" because of its pleasant weather all year round. The country is famous for its Mayan culture, traditions, and archaeological sites like Tikal. Guatemala is also home to more than 30 volcanoes, stunning lakes such as Lake Atitlán, and a great variety of flora and fauna. One of its most iconic products is coffee, which is considered among the best in the world.
